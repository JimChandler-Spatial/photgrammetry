Photo-maps

This animation consists of "ortho-photographs" derived from aerial photographs obtained in May 1976, March 1995, July 2001 and June 2005. These photo-maps are true to scale and by placing cursor on features it is easy to identify whether change has occurred. For example, notice the massive cliff failure that occurred to the west of the site in the winter of 2001, causing rapid cliff recession in an area of previous stability and the consequent new lobes of mud spilling into the sea.
