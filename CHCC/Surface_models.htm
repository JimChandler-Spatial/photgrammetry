Surface models

This animation is derived from thousands of surface points measured at each epoch and processed to form a series of "shaded relief" models. These data were acquired by automated digital photogrammetry (1976, 1995, 2000, 2001-July) and by air-borne laser scanning (1998, 2001- May).

The highest quality surface model was derived in 1995 at a resolution of 1m, all other datasets are at a more coarse resolution of 2m and therefore look a little more blurred. This is most apparent in the excellent depiction of the deep stream gully down the middle of the left-hand lobe.
