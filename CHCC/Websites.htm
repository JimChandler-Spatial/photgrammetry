Websites

The following websites may be of interest:

Jim Chandler's main university website, providing details of related and other research interests/projects.  http://www-staff.lboro.ac.uk/~cvjhc/index.htm
If you would like to find out more about other work and activities carried out by the "Charmouth Heritage Coast Centre". http://www.charmouth.org/

 
If you would like to find out more about photogrammetry, try "The Remote Sensing and Photogrammetry Society". http://www.rspsoc.org/

 
Dr Ian West has produced an excellent website on the Geology of the Dorset Coast (Jurassic Coast, World Heritage Site). http://www.soton.ac.uk/~imw/lymchar.htm http://www.soton.ac.uk/~imw/lymchar.htm#landslide
If you would like to find out more about geomorphology, the study of landforms and their evolution, try the "British Geomorphological Research Group. http://boris.qub.ac.uk/bgrg/

 
Local mapping courtesy of "The Ordnance Survey" http://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/
