Sectional profiles

Below is an animation which demonstrates change that has occurred within a section or plane that cuts through the landslide from the cliff top down to the sea (Plane location). The surfaces used include: 1946, 1958, 1969, 1976, 1988, 2001, approximately every 10 years.

(Note that the line representing the 1946 epoch is "dashed" because the quality of data is lower than other periods).

See how recession of the cliff edge (or rear scarp) progresses at a rate exceeding 1 metre per year, with some substantial variation through time. Also how pulses of mud have spewed out onto the beach and into the sea, only to be eroded and cut back by coastal processes.
